Simplify Cookies Configuration

Easily setup cookie notice that compliant with all major privacy regulations and frameworks

Team: Product Manager, Customer Supports @ OneTrust

Duration: Nov 2019 - Dec 2019

Key Words: Enterprise UX, Desktop Design

My Role: UX Design, Usability Testing

Overview

Project Background

To comply with global privacy regulations, including GDPR from EU and CCPA from California, US, website owners need to provide a notice about the cookies on a website before they are dropped, and need to provide controls of the cookies to site visitors. While the privacy landscape taking over the entire world, an increasing number of organizations of all sizes are using our cookie product to help them prepare for these new laws.

Problem

The current product was originally designed for huge companies with a team of technical and legal person to work with. For most marketers and small business owners who have neither technical nor legal background, the current product is bulky and difficult to use. They have to invest an extra amount of time learning to use the product. Therefore, a better tool is in need enable a more intuitive implementation process.

Research

Even though I was given the prompt, I needed to break down the project space into achievable tasks and tackle them though a solid design process as well as rigorous collaboration.

Defining User Needs

At the outset of the project I didn't have a clear goal but abstarct ideas from product managers. To better understand users’ needs, I scheduled observation sessions as well as focus group discussions with clients of different sizes to answer the following questions.

Who & What roles are involved to setup cookie compliance?
What are users' goals using the tool?
How was the tool support their goals?

After I talked with clients and customer supports, I found people's frustrations generally fell into the following three categories:

1. “I have to go everywhere to change everything.”

Clients complained that even the current tool supports all the functionalities, it's hard to use because all pieces are seperate. Even they just want to make a small change like changing a button color, they have to go to mutiple places to make the change appear on their websites.

2. “I don’t know what’s the next step. How can I put the banner I just created on my website?”

Clients were lost in the configuration process because we didn't provide enough information around where the functions are located and what actions are required to finish the configuration. It's like throwing users into a forest and there is no way out.

3. "I need to be compliant but I know nothing about these privacy laws. What kind of configuration settings should I use?"

Most clients don't have a background in privacy regulations, so it's difficult for them to know what settings are required. And our tool didn't inform users appropriately. Even if some clients have a privacy team in their organizations to consult, it will cause a lot of back and forth.

Current User Flow

To better understand what blocks users from achiving their goals, I audit the current design and found the gaps from the current user flow.

Design Goals

Based on the user needs and the gaps in existing products, I summarized the higher level goals that I hope to achieve with the redesign.

Design

Proposed New User Flow

The ideation starts with a high level user flowchart to collect feedbacks from stakeholders, including developers and produt managers, this has been working well for me to inform the team on my design processes and raise UX awareness for the whole team.

User Feedbacks & Revised Design Goals

All users liked the idea of having a centralized place to manage all configurations and keep the advanced settings at the current locations. This was reassuring for us that the redesign brings value to users. We also received suggestions that added more to our design goals.

- Explain what’s included in a "Container"

It’s too overwhelming to ask users to make the decisions of container at very beginning, especiall for new users who are not familiar with the concept of container.

- Add a Setup Wizard

Based on feedbacks, I created a refined user flow and added a setup wizard in order to educate new users on the concept of "Container". The new flow got greate feebacks from stakeholders.
